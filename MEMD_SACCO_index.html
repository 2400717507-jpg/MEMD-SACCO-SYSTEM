What is the Arrears Report?

The SACCO requires every member to pay a monthly subscription of UGX 30,000 for 12 months each year. A member is placed in arrears when they have not completed all 12 payments.

The outstanding amount owed is calculated as:

(12 months − months already paid) × UGX 30,000 = Amount Owed

For example — if a member has paid for only 5 months, they owe: (12 − 5) × 30,000 = UGX 210,000.

Only members whose months paid is less than 12 appear in this report. All others are fully paid up and active.
